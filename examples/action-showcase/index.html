Why we built it

Demos should not drift every time the product changes.

Live walkthroughs are hard to repeat. Recorded videos go stale. Automation scripts are rarely presenter-friendly.

ScreenReel keeps the product journey in one readable flow so teams can present it, edit it, test it, and record it without rebuilding the story for each format.

How it works

A small contract, not another platform.

1

Author the flow

Describe routes, stable selectors, actions, timing, and talking points in JSON.

2

Present and refine

Mount Projector in the product and use Studio to shape a personal walkthrough.

3

Capture and share

Use the CLI to validate targets, replay scenes, and render the product story.
